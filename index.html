CODING STARTS NOW.

We are Codeward.

Empowering the next generation to code.

Codeward is an organization run by community college students at Chabot College and continues to be led by said community college students. Founded in 2024, Codeward was born from our drive, our passion, and our belief that coding should be accessible to everyone. Coding is a skill that can skyrocket a child's imagination and creativity — and imagination and creativity are what make a childhood awesome!
