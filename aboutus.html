We are a team of community college students at Chabot College who are passionate about coding and education. Our goal is to make coding accessible to everyone, especially children in the East Bay area.

THE FOUNDER & PRESIDENT

DAVID CALARA

David founded Codeward with a few other friends during Chabot College's winter recess. His passion for coding sparked the birth of Codeward. United by a goal to make coding accessible to children in the East Bay Community, what once was a small idea turned into a student-led initiative hosting coding workshops, hackathons, and community events with him at its center. David is also a tutor at Chabot College's Learning Connection, tutoring students in computer science and math. He is also an officer for Chabot's Computer Science Club.
