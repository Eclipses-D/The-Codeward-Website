WE'RE ON A MISSION TO MAKE CODING ACCESSIBLE TO EVERYONE.

OUR MISSION

Coding makes up our lives one way or another. Whether that'll be work, school or personal time. Our mission is to encourage the youth to take on coding and pave a path forward to them that makes an impact not only in coding but also the world. In Codeward, we believe that every child should have the ability code. Coding invokes creativity, critical thinking and imagination all in a computer!

WHAT DO WE OFFER?

We offer opportunity to children and parents alike!

Here are the types of services we offer:

WORKSHOPS

Codeward hosts workshops throughout the Hayward area and neighboring cities such as: San Lorenzo, Union City & San Leandro. We host workshops at libraries, community and youth centers. In the workshops, we focus on a curriculum that creates a fun and intentive learning environment. The best part: All of this is for free!

HACKATHONS

Hackathons are a coding competition that are open to everyone who into coding. Our hackathons are no exception! We host hackathons as a well our Codeward partcipants to hone their skills, meet their peers and most importantly, have fun!

TYPES OF CODING LANGUAGES

Scratch

For younger students and those who are new to coding, Scratch will be primary used for that demographic. Scratch is a visual progamming language developed by MIT. It is a very beginner friendly language and an great introduction to those who are interested in coding. Scratch uses colorful drag and drop code blocks, allowing stories, games, animations to be made without tradition code!

Python

For older students and those who have coding experience. Python is the world's most commonly used langauge and used widely in big technology companies: Google, Netflix, Meta, Uber, Spotify, Reddit and many more. Python is a beginner friendly language known for its easy readable code. Our workshops get students to apply Python to make interactive games!
