OVERVIEW OF CODEWARD WORKSHOPS

TAKE A LOOK AT OUR WORKSHOPS

Join us for our upcoming workshops! We offer a variety of coding workshops designed to inspire creativity and foster a love for technology in children. Whether your child is a beginner or has some coding experience, our classes provide a fun and engaging environment to learn and create.

Codeward: Make Your Own Chase Game

Learn how to make games and animations with Scratch! No prior experience required. Bring your creativity to life on screen using simple drag-and-drop blocks. In this fun, beginner-friendly program, kids age 8-12 will turn their ideas into projects while building problem-solving and critical-thinking skills. Led by the dynamic team from Codeward!

This session’s project: Make Your Own Chase Game! Scratch accounts will be provided by the educators.

Date: July 25, 2025

Time: 1:00pm - 2:30pm

Location: Hayward Public Library
